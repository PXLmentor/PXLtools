## Goal
Expand the PXLsuite tools surface alongside the HIGH-priority PXLbid/PXLflow/PXLrv trio. PXLsuite as a whole is the lever for Cris's pivot from VFX/CG Supervisor to consultant — PXLbid and PXLflow are already attracting client interest, so adding more solid tools under the same umbrella amplifies that pull. Two concrete priorities right now: a Maya↔Unreal asset bridge (built against the Thresholds workflow), and a clean drag-and-drop installer plus auto-updater for the existing tools (Turntable, GLB Manager).
